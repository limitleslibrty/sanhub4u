"Ajey: The Untold Story of a Yogi" is a biographical film based on the life of Yogi Adityanath, the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh. It is inspired by the author Shantanu Gupta's book "The Monk who became Chief Minister" .The story delves into his transformation from a young man with humble beginnings to a prominent spiritual and political leader. The film explores his early years, his spiritual journey, and how he became a revered figure in the Hindu community.
